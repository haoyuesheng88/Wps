# Case Catalog

## Supported Presets

### `sales`
- Monthly sales quantities, unit price, revenue formula, and total row.
- Best for: generic sales demo tables and quick business examples.

### `inventory`
- Item code, stock, safety stock, reorder suggestion, and status formula.
- Best for: warehouse or spare-parts stock sheets.

### `attendance`
- Employee attendance days, leave days, lateness count, and attendance rate.
- Best for: HR or workshop attendance examples.

### `expense`
- Reimbursement log with amount totals and approval status.
- Best for: office finance or travel-expense examples.

### `permission-matrix`
- Role-rights matrix with checkmarks highlighted in green and placeholders dimmed.
- Best for: screenshots of permissions, access rights, or operator-role capability tables.

## Selection Rule

- Use `sales` when the user wants a generic example and gives no stronger clue.
- Use `permission-matrix` for screenshots similar to role/permission cross tables.
- Prefer the closest preset over inventing a new one on the fly.
